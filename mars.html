MARS

Mars is the epic travel destination for all space tourists. It's nearly earthly environment and great tourist attractions mean that you are in for an absolutely great visual treat.

The first stop on our tour is Mars' North Pole, which includes an ice cap bigger than Texas. Later on the trip, we'll visit Olympus Mons, the tallest volcano in the solar system, which is three times as tall as Mount Everest. We'll also visit Valles Marines, the largest canyon in the solar system, which runs the length of New York to Los Angeles.
